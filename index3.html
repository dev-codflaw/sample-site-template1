Our Services

🎧

Audiometers

Advanced devices for measuring hearing thresholds across different frequencies, essential for diagnosing hearing loss and evaluating hearing aid effectiveness.

📊

Impedance Audiometry

Specialized equipment measuring middle ear function by assessing tympanic membrane mobility, helping diagnose conductive hearing loss.

🔊

OAE Testing

Otoacoustic Emissions equipment for assessing cochlear function and screening newborns and infants for hearing loss with precision.

🧠

BERA Systems

Brain Evoked Response Auditory systems measuring brainstem electrical activity in response to auditory stimuli for comprehensive diagnosis.
